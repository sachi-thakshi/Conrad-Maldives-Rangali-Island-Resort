Welcome to Conrad Maldives Rangali Island

Nestled in the South Ari Atoll, Conrad Maldives Rangali Island is a luxury resort spanning two private islands connected by a 500-meter bridge. With pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and world-class amenities, our resort offers an unforgettable Maldivian experience.

From our iconic underwater restaurant to our award-winning overwater spa, every moment at Conrad Maldives is designed to create lasting memories.
